NoAuth Version 1

The use of NoAuth version 1 is simple. For any case where the user needs to provide credentials, assume they are correct. This is the basis for a trusting relationship. Who's going to know the user's credentials better than themselves?

As you can see in the example app below, we provide a simple combobox full of credentials to allow the user to find their account more quickly. After that, they can simply login and access their profile. It's that simple!
